# Highest-ROI Persona

## Primary buyer-builder

Senior .NET consultant, solutions architect, or lead engineer whose value comes from delivering practical business software faster than the client expected.

## Their environment

They are usually working inside or adjacent to:

- ERP and CRM systems
- CAD or manufacturing workflows
- line-of-business APIs
- internal admin tools
- migration and integration projects
- operational software with ugly data edges

## How they win

- they reuse trusted utility patterns across engagements
- they spend less time on repeated scaffolding
- they make boring but necessary engineering moves faster
- they hand off clean code that internal client teams can maintain
- they preserve more time for high-margin problem solving

## What they need from this repo

- helpers that remove repeated work quickly
- practical examples that feel production-minded
- clear naming and documentation
- visible technical taste and seriousness on first contact
- a repo they can borrow from, justify, and build on
