# About Thomas Smith

Thomas Smith is the creator of Million Dollar Dot Net Snippets and the founder behind [tsmithcode.ai](https://www.tsmithcode.ai).

He builds software at the point where engineering systems, business operations, and hard-to-integrate workflows collide. His background combines 7+ years delivering production .NET systems with 12+ years in CAD and engineering workflows, which makes the framework unusually grounded in real delivery pressure instead of abstract utility design.

## Why this framework exists

The framework comes from repeated work in environments where speed matters, mistakes are expensive, and system boundaries are messy:

- Autodesk ecosystems including Inventor, AutoCAD, and Vault
- API-led integrations and ETL pipelines
- governed reporting, metadata, lineage, and auditability
- ERP, warehouse, engineering, and shop-floor connectivity
- internal business software that has to work in production, not just demo well

Million Dollar Dot Net Snippets reflects that operating model. It is designed for consultants, architects, and technical leads who need reusable leverage across client work, internal tools, integrations, and operational software.
