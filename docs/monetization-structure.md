# Monetization Structure

This document defines the recommended commercialization path for Million Dollar Dot Net Snippets after the premium product surface and proof layers are in place.

## Recommended structure

Use a three-layer model:

- open core
- premium modules
- strategic advisory

## Layer 1: Open core

Ship the public framework, proof surfaces, docs, and guided wizard openly.

Purpose:

- maximize trust
- build distribution
- reinforce founder authority
- make the product easy to inspect before purchase or engagement

## Layer 2: Premium modules

Reserve the highest-leverage enterprise additions for paid access.

Candidate paid surfaces:

- flagship integration packs
- enterprise packaging accelerators
- deeper rules and diagnostics modules
- governance and deployment templates
- vertical slices for ERP, manufacturing, warehouse, or line-of-business delivery

Purpose:

- monetize the strongest ROI paths
- create clearer upgrade logic
- ensure the best enterprise proof becomes productized, not only custom

## Layer 3: Strategic advisory

Use the framework as the operating system behind premium founder-led services.

Advisory positioning:

- modernization and reuse strategy
- enterprise integration acceleration
- governed internal platform work
- high-value delivery rescue and architecture guidance

Purpose:

- monetize immediately without waiting for pure software traction
- turn the framework into a service multiplier
- compound product quality through real delivery feedback

## Why this structure has the best ROI

- it preserves public credibility
- it avoids paywalling the proof layer too early
- it creates a natural upsell path for serious buyers
- it fits the founder's current authority and likely near-term market entry

## What should not happen yet

- do not over-fragment the offer into too many SKUs
- do not hide the best proof behind private access before trust is built
- do not claim enterprise monetization readiness before packaging and flagship proof improve further
