# Premium Pass Checklist

Use this checklist for CEO-visible quality reviews.

## Product clarity

- the repo title and hero communicate a sharp promise
- the target persona is obvious in under 10 seconds
- there is one clear path to first success

## Quality of presentation

- assets feel intentional and premium
- the repo does not read like scattered internal leftovers
- navigation has a clear hierarchy
- examples feel like proof, not filler

## Accessibility

- SVGs include meaningful titles and descriptions
- contrast is strong enough for comfortable reading
- section ordering makes sense when read linearly
- alt text and linked labels are specific

## Enterprise readiness

- solution and project entrypoints are obvious
- docs and contribution surface are complete
- support, conduct, and security expectations are visible
- public claims are backed by buildable proof
