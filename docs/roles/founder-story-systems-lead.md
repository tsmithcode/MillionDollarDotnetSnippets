# Founder Story Systems Lead

## Mission

Turn the creator narrative into a trusted product asset that strengthens the framework rather than distracting from it.

## Owns

- founder/about page direction
- biography and credibility narrative
- the bridge between founder history and product truth
- case-study selection for authority and trust

## First 30 days

- turn the founder story into a concise, evidence-backed public page
- align the story with the highest-ROI persona
- remove vague founder language that does not help trust or conversion

## Scorecard

- the founder page feels credible, not self-congratulatory
- users understand why this creator is qualified to build the framework
- the narrative reinforces product trust and adoption
