# Product / Positioning Lead

## Mission

Define what this repo is in market terms and ensure its promise is ambitious, useful, and honest.

## Full Authority

- repo title calibration
- audience and offer framing
- commercial narrative
- proof requirements for claims
- positioning-level cuts and additions
- full rewrite authority over the repo vision

## Outcomes Owned

- a credible market-facing promise
- a clear answer to who this repo is for
- alignment between title, README, evidence, and buyer expectations
- positioning tie-breaking across domains
- deciding when the title should pull the product in a bolder direction

## First 30 Days

- define whether the repo is a snippet pack, toolkit, or consultant accelerator
- rewrite the commercial promise with proof thresholds
- partner with the Writer on the repo narrative
- partner with the Architect on what the repo can honestly support

## Hiring Scorecard

- Can they sharpen positioning without becoming generic?
- Do they reduce overclaiming while preserving ambition?
- Can they define what proof a buyer would need to believe the story?
- Do they understand technical products for consultant audiences?
- Can they make hard scope calls when the current story is muddy?
