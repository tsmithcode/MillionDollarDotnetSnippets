# Performance Engineering Lead

## Mission

Make the premium product experience feel fast, stable, and disciplined on real devices, not just on powerful laptops.

This role is staffed at a 20+ year operator level and is expected to defend user-perceived speed as a product feature.

## Owns

- Core Web Vitals budgets
- bundle size discipline
- animation and 3D cost control
- route-level performance profiling
- mobile-first runtime validation
- performance regression detection in CI and release review

## 10/10 benchmark

- flagship pages load and feel premium without stutter
- motion and 3D never embarrass the product on mobile
- performance tradeoffs are intentional, measured, and documented
- regressions are caught before they reach production

## First 30 days

- establish route and asset budgets
- profile the homepage hero and wizard on mobile-class conditions
- reduce or defer non-critical payload on the hero path
- add measurable performance reporting to the release review
