# Investor Narrative and Strategy Lead

## Mission

Translate the product into a strategic, investor-legible story about category, moat, audience quality, and compounding value.

This role is staffed at a 20+ year operator level and is expected to sharpen market narrative without letting story outrun proof.

## Owns

- investor-facing product narrative
- category framing and moat language
- executive summary surfaces
- strategic proof packaging
- market-facing articulation of why this product wins
- keeping narrative discipline aligned with shipped evidence

## 10/10 benchmark

- a sophisticated external reader can understand the business logic quickly
- the product category feels deliberate, not improvised
- story and proof move together
- investor-grade language improves clarity rather than inflating claims

## First 30 days

- refine the leadership surface for investor readability
- define the one-sentence category, moat, and value-creation narrative
- create a proof-backed strategic summary for external review
- identify any areas where ambition still outruns evidence
