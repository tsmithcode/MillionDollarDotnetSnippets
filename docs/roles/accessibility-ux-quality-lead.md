# Accessibility & UX Quality Lead

## Mission

Make the repo easy to use, readable, and trustworthy for a wide range of users, including assistive-technology users and executives scanning quickly.

## Full Authority

- accessibility standards for docs and assets
- scannability and readability review
- navigation clarity
- premium-pass usability checks across README, docs, and examples

## Outcomes Owned

- accessible visual assets
- clearer navigation and onboarding
- fewer comprehension gaps in the repo surface
- confidence that premium design is not trading away usability
