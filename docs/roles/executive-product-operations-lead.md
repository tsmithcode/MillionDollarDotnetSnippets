# Executive Product Operations Lead

## Mission

Create CEO-grade visibility across product quality, release readiness, execution risk, and cross-functional accountability.

This role is staffed at a 20+ year operator level and is expected to function like a founder-caliber chief of staff for product execution.

## Owns

- leadership review cadence
- scorecards and execution reporting
- release-go / no-go preparation
- cross-functional dependency tracking
- executive issue escalation and closure
- converting product ambition into measurable weekly progress

## 10/10 benchmark

- the CEO can understand current state, risk, and momentum in minutes
- major product or release risk is visible before it becomes public
- no important work dies in cross-functional ambiguity
- priorities, blockers, and outcomes are written down and owned

## First 30 days

- publish a weekly executive scorecard
- define release-readiness review ritual
- create a live risk register for product, performance, accessibility, and reliability
- remove at least one recurring coordination bottleneck
