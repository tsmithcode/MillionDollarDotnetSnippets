# CEO Execution Matrix

This document is the CEO-visible execution scorecard for MillionDollarDotnetSnippets.

All roles in this matrix are expected to operate at a 20+ year specialist level.
Scores use a 10-point scale:

- `current score`: where the product stands today based on shipped evidence
- `expected outcome score`: where the product should land after the named owner and supporting experts complete the next execution pass

## Executive Summary

The product is now strong enough to present with confidence, but not yet finished enough to coast.

The biggest remaining uplift comes from:

- performance discipline
- trust and enterprise posture
- executive operating visibility
- investor-grade strategic narrative

## Added CEO-Visible Experts

### Executive Product Operations Lead

- purpose: make execution status obvious to the CEO and keep cross-functional work moving
- success signal: the leadership team can see current score, target score, blockers, and release risk every week

### Performance Engineering Lead

- purpose: make the product feel elite on real devices, not just look elite in screenshots
- success signal: flagship pages remain premium while materially lowering performance cost

### Security and Trust Lead

- purpose: turn enterprise trust into a visible, measurable product quality dimension
- success signal: trust posture becomes legible to enterprise buyers and part of every release review

### Investor Narrative and Strategy Lead

- purpose: make the business logic and moat legible without inflating claims
- success signal: strategic readers immediately understand category, audience quality, and why this product compounds
