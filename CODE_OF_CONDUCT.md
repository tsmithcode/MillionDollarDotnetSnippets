# Code of Conduct

We expect a high-trust, high-standards working environment.

## Our standard

- be direct and respectful
- critique work, not people
- prefer clarity over posture
- keep the repo honest

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- dismissive or degrading language
- knowingly misrepresenting shipped capabilities
- undermining collaborators through gatekeeping or status games
